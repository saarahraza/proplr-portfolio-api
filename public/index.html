AI Integration — Weeks 4–8

Enablement & Handoff — Weeks 9–12

Company Values · Brand Truth

Proplr brand values

Every deliverable in this portfolio was crafted to align with Proplr's core operating principles — not just their services.

🔩

Delivery over decks

Proplr's value is in shipping, not slides. Every message is grounded in what the team actually builds, not what they promise.

🎯

Precision over volume

Fractional by design — the model demands specificity. No bloat, no padding. Every hour and every word earns its place.

📐

Outcomes over outputs

ROI isn't a promise, it's a starting point. Vaultly's $180K in savings is the floor, not the ceiling — and it's tracked, reported, and disclosed transparently.

🤝

Embedded, not external

Proplr doesn't consult from afar. They join the team, learn the codebase, and ship alongside the client — more like a hire than an agency.

🔁

Continuous improvement

Every engagement is iterated. QA cycles, feedback loops, and post-handoff check-ins mean the work gets better after it ships, not just before.

💡

Clarity as a service

Technical complexity is only valuable if stakeholders understand it. Proplr translates cloud architecture into language that boards, founders, and sales teams can act on.

🔬 Tool Evaluation · Research

AI tool comparison

How Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity were evaluated for this project — and which won each job.

Claude Content pipeline

Best tone consistency and instruction-following. Strongest for brand-voice writing with complex constraints.

Perplexity Market research

Best for real-time signals — competitor hiring data, keyword trends, and sourcing landscape research with live citations.

Gemini Data & structured output

Strong for Google Workspace integration and structured data tasks. Natural fit for a Google Cloud partner context.

ChatGPT Brainstorming

Useful for ideation and broad first drafts. Less consistent on specific brand voice — better as a starting point than a finisher.
